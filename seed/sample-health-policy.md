# Sample Health Insurance Policy (SYNTHETIC — for testing only)

## Coverage Summary
This synthetic policy is for CLAIRO testing. It does not represent a real insurance product.

## Covered Services
- Office/outpatient visits (procedure codes 99201–99215) are covered when medically necessary.
- Preventive screenings (procedure codes 99381–99397) are covered at 100%.
- Diagnostic laboratory tests are covered when ordered by a licensed provider.

## Exclusions
- Cosmetic procedures are not covered.
- Experimental or investigational treatments are excluded unless pre-authorized.
- Services rendered outside the coverage period are excluded.

## Limits
- Annual out-of-pocket maximum applies.
- Prior authorization is required for procedures exceeding EUR 2,000.

## Claim Adjudication Notes
- A claim is APPROVED when the procedure is covered, within the coverage period, and no exclusion applies.
- A claim is DENIED when an exclusion applies or the service is outside the coverage period.
- A claim is PARTIAL when only some line items are covered.
- Use NEEDS_MORE_INFO when required documentation (e.g., prior authorization) is missing.
